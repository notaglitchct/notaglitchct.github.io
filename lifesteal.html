Welcome to Lifesteal 2 German

Lifesteal 2 German is a Minecraft server that is a continuation of the original LifeSteal server. When a player kills you, He gets the Heart. But if you die from a Mob or Fall Damage, your Heart is gone.

This is the German/Deutsche Version of Lifesteal 2. (Lifesteal 2 German)

To join the server, you need to have the Java Edition of Minecraft and Discord.
